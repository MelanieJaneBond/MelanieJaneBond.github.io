Professional Software Developer

in Nashville, TN

An enthusiastic Team Player and determined Developer, I discovered programming while I was working with one-year-olds and I couldn’t be happier to apply my fascination with complex systems to the world of software.

For years, I was dreaming of a place where the learning never ends, teamwork really is what makes the dream work, and the products being produced go farther than the grams of deliciousness in a chocolate chip cookie; it seems I found my job-promised-land in Software Development.

After several years in service industries (baking and teaching young children), I began to wonder whether there was a way I could serve on a larger scale, I took an interest in how successful websites are run and why. Some online study showed me programs I could begin on my own, but, I had a friend working in Software who advised me to find a reliable local school where the instructors could help me make sure I was learning everything the right way. I submitted an application to Nashville Software School later that month and here I am today, building apps in React and learning Python.

Building tools that meet needs and solve problems brings me great joy! It's also a perk that in development, we work in teams much of the time; people are by far my favorite complex organisms.
